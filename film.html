We offer a broad spectrum of motion picture editing services, including -

General Movie Editing

Our general film editing is among the most basic yet professional movie editing service which includes sequencing the footages that you provide for editing to ensure that your videos narrate a story in the right sequence. We also merge different videos into one standardized video format, removing any blurred, shaky and unwanted scenes.

Special Effects Addition

Along with the editing of the videos, we also enhance your raw footages with a plethora of special effects such as addition of background scores, animations, voice-overs, changing the appearance of certain scenes with different colors, tilting & zooming, transitions, and the addition of titles in the beginning or ending of the video.

Movie Enhancement with Background Score Integration

Most videos often carry sounds and background voices which may be distracting and unwanted. We provide our clients with the high-quality background score integration services to enhance the quality and appeal of the raw footages, and give them a professional outlook. We add the background music that aptly matches the content of the video and makes it even more interesting & appealing.

Image Stabilization and Color Enhancement Services

A lot of times, when the movies are shot, every scene may not be captured the way the movie maker intended it to be. As a result, some images or scenes appear blurred, shaky or unclear. We help our clients with the most advanced image stabilization and color enhancement services to remove any distracting or low quality shots, and correct the colors which may have been affected by bad weather conditions, low light or any other external factors.

Subtitling and Annotations

If you have been looking for a film editing service where you can get the titles, subtitles and annotations added in the movie for the convenience of the viewers, we can provide the subtitles and titles in any language, font, and style.
